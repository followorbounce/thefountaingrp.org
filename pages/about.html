Our approach to care

We integrate lifestyle medicine into our practice, focusing on a holistic and biological approach to mental well-being. Cash patients receive extended consultation times for a more in-depth understanding of their needs.

The practice prioritizes accuracy — utilizing the most evidence-based methods to ensure precise diagnoses without rushing the process. Both psychotherapy (talk therapy) and medication management are available, all through HIPAA-compliant telehealth for complete privacy and security.

The Fountain Group operates across three locations — Reston, VA; Oakland, CA; and Honolulu, HI — making high-quality psychiatric care accessible across the country. All appointment types are also available via HIPAA-compliant telehealth, open seven days a week from 8am to 9pm.
